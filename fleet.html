Our Fleet

Our Fleet size

Our fleet size is comprised of well-equipped Toyota Land Cruisers and Land Rovers 4x4’s with either extra-large sliding windows or canvas windows and pop up roofs which enable the best animal viewing while safety is observed.

The vehicles are well maintained and well cleaned after and before each safari.

They’ve a rear passenger capacity of six people and forward capacity of two people one being the driver.

The most aft compartment is the luggage are capable of holding all occupants luggage and a mini fridge for on board refreshments

Also on board our safari vehicles are charging port to make sure you don’t miss a moment while adventuring in the wild, please remember to bring your phone’s charger

Emergency tools onboard

On board First aid kit is mandatory before the beginning of the adventure.

Also emergency VHF radio is available in case of a total communication failure.

What happens in case of breakdown?

In case of a breakdown emergency that can’t be fixed on the road immediately we will organize with immediate effect a rescue vehicles

We do avoid and maintain a good look out throughout the safari but due to, though rarely roughness and nature of the adventure the vehicles do sustain a few pounding.
